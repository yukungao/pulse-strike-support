Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Pulse Strike handles gameplay data, platform services, and support communications in the current release.

Effective Date
March 22, 2026

1. Who We Are

This app is published by Dexter. If you have questions about this policy or about the way the app handles data, contact us at support@example.com.

2. Information Stored On Your Device

The app stores gameplay-related information locally on your device so core features work properly. This may include settings, audio and haptics preferences, progression state, chapter unlocks, best score, accessibility preferences, and other local game state required to preserve your experience between sessions.

3. iCloud Best Score Sync

The current release can use Apple's iCloud key-value storage to sync best-score information across your devices when iCloud is available and enabled for your Apple account. This sync is handled through Apple's platform services rather than a custom game account system.

4. Game Center

If you choose to sign in with Game Center, Apple may process your Game Center profile and submitted scores so the leaderboard feature can work. We do not create a separate in-game account for this feature.

5. In-App Purchases

If you buy or restore the non-consumable Remove Ads purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card information. We may receive transaction state information needed to unlock or restore the purchase inside the app.

6. What The App Does Not Currently Collect Directly

Based on the current shipping code path, the app does not currently use a custom account system, direct sign-up flow, third-party ad SDK, third-party analytics SDK, or custom marketing tracker. If that changes in a future release, this privacy policy should be updated before that version is distributed.

7. Support Messages

If you contact support, we will receive the information you choose to send us, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, and the contents of your message. We use that information only to respond to your request, troubleshoot the issue, and improve the stability or usability of the app.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by Apple when you use platform services such as Game Center, iCloud, or In-App Purchases. Those services are governed by Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

9. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you remove the app or clear the data through platform-level means. Support messages are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and keep basic support records.

10. Your Choices

You can choose whether to sign into Game Center. You can also manage iCloud availability at the Apple account level on your device. If you no longer want the app to keep local gameplay data on the device, deleting the app will typically remove local app storage subject to Apple's platform behavior.

11. Children's Privacy

The app is a general-audience arcade game and is not intended to collect personal information directly from children. If you believe support communications or another interaction unintentionally provided personal information from a child, contact us so we can review and address the request appropriately.

12. Changes To This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time if the app's features, connected platform services, or legal obligations change. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
