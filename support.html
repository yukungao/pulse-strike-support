Before You Email

  1. Restart the app after changing language, login, or purchase state.
  2. Confirm your device is signed into Game Center if leaderboard access is failing.
  3. Use the in-game Restore Purchases option if your non-consumable purchase is missing.
  4. Make sure iCloud is enabled on the same Apple account if your best score is not syncing.

What To Include

  • Your device model and iOS version.
  • The game version you are running.
  • The exact issue and what you expected to happen.
  • Whether the issue happens every time or only sometimes.
  • A screenshot or screen recording if you can provide one.

Supported Languages

The game currently includes English, Simplified Chinese, Spanish, Japanese, and Korean UI support. Support requests may still be answered primarily in English.

Common Help Paths

Leaderboard Not Loading

Confirm that Game Center is enabled for your Apple account in iOS Settings, then relaunch the app. If the problem continues, contact support and include whether the system sign-in sheet appeared.

Remove Ads Purchase

Open Settings in the game and use Restore Purchases. If the purchase still does not appear, send support your storefront region and the device you used when buying the item.

Language Or UI Issue

Mention the language you selected, whether the issue happens in menus or gameplay, and attach a screenshot. This helps us trace missing localization or layout regressions faster.
